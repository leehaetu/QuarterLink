# HMRC Facts

## Standard Update Periods and Deadlines

| Quarter | Standard period | Deadline |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Q1 | 6 April to 5 July | 7 August |
| Q2 | 6 April to 5 October | 7 November |
| Q3 | 6 April to 5 January | 7 February |
| Q4 | 6 April to 5 April | 7 May |

## Rules

- Period end date and deadline are different.
- Q1 period ends 5 July.
- Q1 deadline is 7 August.
- Do not confuse the period end date with the deadline.
- Quarterly updates are not tax returns.
- Final declaration / tax return features are out of MVP.
- Support standard update periods first.
- Calendar update periods are a later feature.
- The £90,000 VAT registration threshold affects category detail where allowed, but QuarterLink is not building VAT features.
